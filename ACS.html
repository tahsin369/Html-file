Sharoare Hosan Emon

Sharoare Hosan Emon is a notable figure in the educational technology sector in Bangladesh. He graduated with a degree in Biomedical Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 2020. Emon is the Co-Founder and CEO of Ronon, an ed-tech startup aimed at improving online education accessibility and quality in Bangladesh​ (Ronon)​​ (HSC Crackers)​. Emon is also deeply involved with ACS (Apar's Classroom), an educational platform that provides resources and support for students. He is known for his work with ACS Doubts, a platform that helps students get solutions to their academic questions quickly and effectively​ (ACS Doubts)​. His contributions extend to HSC Crackers, another educational initiative where he emphasizes making premium education accessible to all students, regardless of their financial background​ (HSC Crackers)​. Through his various roles, Emon is committed to leveraging technology to make education more inclusive and effective, demonstrating a strong dedication to educational innovation and support for students across Bangladesh.
