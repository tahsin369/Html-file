Numeri Sattar Apar

Numeri Sattar Apar is a prominent figure in the Bangladeshi educational technology sector. He is a Co-Founder and the CEO of Apar's Classroom (ACS), an organization dedicated to providing quality educational resources. Apar holds a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). He also serves as the Managing Director of the ACS Group, where he focuses on leveraging technology to enhance educational access and effectiveness for students in Bangladesh​ (Apar's Classroom)​.
