Avi Dutta Tushar

Avi Dutta Tushar is a prominent figure associated with Apar's Classroom (ACS), a leading educational platform in Bangladesh. He completed his Mechanical Engineering degree from the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) in 2015. With over eight years of experience as a math instructor, Tushar has mentored numerous students and has established himself as an expert in higher mathematics​ (Apar's Classroom)​​ (YouTube)​. Tushar is known for his engaging and comprehensive teaching style, particularly in higher mathematics for HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) students. He runs multiple courses, including specialized batches for different academic cycles, such as the Higher Math 1st Paper and 2nd Paper cycles. His courses cover a wide range of topics from basic concepts to advanced techniques necessary for competitive exams​ (Apar's Classroom)​​ (Apar's Classroom)​. In addition to his work with ACS, Tushar is the founder of the RhomBus Parallel Science Hub, which further underscores his commitment to enhancing educational outcomes for students through innovative teaching methods​ (Apar's Classroom)​.
